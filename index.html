It additionally provided paid licenses to use in advertising, promo, radio, DVD, etc. You may know of this service solely for housing two infamous songs. The service has rebranded and appeared to have rebooted in recent times and unfortunately, resulted in songs disappearing, likely due to expired licensing. God-knows-who have managed to save a majority of the songs from then using the Wayback Machine, but has left captures totally scattered and, obviously, has no way to do exact or humane searches that's not clicking in and out of category pages. So, here I bring you an extensive search engine for that. Because of current Internet times, the original stated terms may or may not apply or be enforced as heavily today, like other decade old copyright situations.
